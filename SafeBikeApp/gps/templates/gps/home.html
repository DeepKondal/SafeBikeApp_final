Check bike status

Check the current status of the bike. Employing sensor fusion technology, the bike is equipped with advanced sensors like accelerometers, gyroscopes, and GPS modules. The backend processes real-time data, to determine if the bike is stationary or in motion.

Set recording interval

The "Set Intervals" page is a crucial feature that empowers users to customize their GPS recording experience. By allowing users to choose specific intervals, they can tailor the recording time based on their preferences and needs. This flexibility enhances app usability .
